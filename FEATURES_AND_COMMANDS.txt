================================================================================
QUANT TOOLKIT - FEATURES AND COMMANDS
================================================================================

Research only. Not investment advice. No live brokerage trading is performed.

Run commands from the project root:

  python3 cli.py <command> [options]

Data:
  - Prices: Yahoo Finance
  - Filing-risk scores: cached in tenk_cache.json
  - Default portfolio universe: semis
  - Default portfolio momentum preset: mom_10d
  - Default headline transaction cost: 10 bps round-trip


================================================================================
1. BEST DEFAULT: COMPREHENSIVE UNIVERSE ANALYSIS
================================================================================

Use this when you want the broadest single report for any universe:

  python3 cli.py portfolio compare --universe <universe_name> --years 10 --momentum-preset mom_10d --cost-bps 10 --validate

Semis example:

  python3 cli.py portfolio compare --universe semis --years 10 --momentum-preset mom_10d --cost-bps 10 --validate

Custom ticker example:

  python3 cli.py portfolio compare --tickers AMAT,ASX,COHR,INTC,LRCX,GFS,ARM,AMD,MRVL,ALAB --years 10 --momentum-preset mom_10d --cost-bps 10 --validate

This command is the most complete one-stop analysis. It includes:
  - Data quality and coverage checks
  - Strategy comparison:
      1. equal-weight benchmark
      2. cross-sectional momentum long-only
      3. cross-sectional momentum long/short
      4. combined signal long-only
  - Current portfolio ranking and combined signal report
  - Single-stock mean-reversion and momentum confirmation
  - Cached annual filing risk: 10-K or 20-F
  - Cached quarterly filing risk: 10-Q, where available
  - Foreign issuer filing labels such as "20-F only (no 10-Q)"
  - Risk contribution and covariance-based sizing review
  - Walk-forward out-of-sample validation
  - Equal-weight benchmark comparison
  - Transaction-cost sensitivity at multiple bps levels
  - Final gated conclusion

Important:
  - The command reads existing filing scores from tenk_cache.json.
  - It does not refresh SEC filings or call the LLM by itself.
  - Refresh filing scores separately with tenk_reader.py when needed.

================================================================================
3. FILING-RISK COMMANDS
================================================================================

Filing scores are cached and then merged into portfolio reports.

Annual filings:
  - US companies: 10-K
  - Foreign issuers: 20-F

Quarterly filings:
  - US companies: 10-Q
  - Foreign issuers usually do not have 10-Q coverage

Refresh annual and quarterly filing scores for a universe:

  python3 tenk_reader.py --universe semis --filing-type both --force

Refresh annual 10-K/20-F only:

  python3 tenk_reader.py --universe semis --filing-type annual --force

Refresh quarterly 10-Q only:

  python3 tenk_reader.py --universe semis --filing-type quarterly --force

Refresh one ticker:

  python3 tenk_reader.py ASX --filing-type annual --force
  python3 tenk_reader.py INTC --filing-type quarterly --force

Debug extraction without LLM scoring:

  python3 tenk_reader.py --debug-extract ASX --filing-type annual
  python3 tenk_reader.py --debug-extract INTC --filing-type quarterly

Note:
  - Scoring requires ANTHROPIC_API_KEY.
  - Debug extraction does not require paid LLM calls.
  - Portfolio reports use cached scores; stale cache means stale filing-risk columns.

================================================================================
5. MODEL SUMMARY
================================================================================

Cross-sectional momentum:
  Ranks stocks relative to the selected universe. Longs are top-ranked names;
  shorts or avoids are bottom-ranked names, depending on command mode.

Single-stock momentum:
  Confirms whether a ticker has positive time-series momentum on its own.

Mean reversion:
  Uses z-score versus a rolling mean to detect stretched or oversold conditions.

Combined signal:
  Merges cross-sectional rank, single-stock momentum, and mean-reversion state
  into BUY, WAIT, WATCH, AVOID, or SHORT_CANDIDATE.

Filing-risk overlay:
  Uses cached annual 10-K/20-F and quarterly 10-Q scores. A BUY can be flagged
  with risk conflict if filing language worsened beyond the threshold.

Risk sizing:
  Estimates covariance on coverage-approved names and reports equal-weight risk
  contribution plus alternative sizing methods such as risk parity.

Validation:
  Walk-forward validation checks whether the strategy has out-of-sample edge
  versus an equal-weight benchmark after realistic transaction costs.

================================================================================
9. TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Unknown universe:
  Run `python3 cli.py portfolio universes`.

Invalid command option:
  Use `--cost-bps 10`, not `--10bps`.

No filing data:
  Refresh tenk_cache.json with tenk_reader.py. Foreign issuers may only have
  20-F annual filings and no 10-Q quarterly filings.

Stale filing score:
  Re-run tenk_reader.py with --force.

Insufficient history:
  Use --years 10 for validation-heavy work. Young tickers may be shown as
  insufficient history and excluded from validation or risk sizing.

Yahoo Finance errors:
  Check ticker spelling, network access, and rate limits.
